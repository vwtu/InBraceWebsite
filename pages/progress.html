Progress Records Uploader

**Please note that this page is NOT for initial case submission. Initial case submissions should be done through our InBrace Virtual Office (VO) Submission Portal**

Early access users are asked to upload progress records for clinical training purposes. As described in the User Guide, please provide the following:

Intraoral photographs

  • At least one progress intra-oral scan is recommended after the initial alignment stage
  • At least one progress panoramic X-Ray is recommended during the early finishing stage
  • Additional progress records per the doctor's discretion

Please ensure to describe the following when uploading patient files:

  1. InBrace Username/Email
  2. Patient name
  3. Visit date
  4. Record Submitted (Progress, Finishing or Bonding Record)

Note: Please wait until all files have completed uploading before leaving the page.
